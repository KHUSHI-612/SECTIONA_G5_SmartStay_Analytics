# 📊 SmartStay Analytics: Albany Market Performance Report
**DVA Capstone 2 Project | Section A, Group 5**

---

## 1. Executive Summary
The short-term rental market in Albany, NY, is a unique blend of legislative activity, educational demand, and tourism. This report analyzes over 7,500 data points to identify the key drivers of revenue and guest satisfaction in the capital region. 

**Key Findings:**
*   **Neighborhood Alpha:** The 6th and 13th Wards consistently outperform the city average in occupancy.
*   **The Superhost Premium:** Statistical analysis confirms that Superhost status leads to a **12% higher RevPAR**.
*   **Capacity Sweet Spot:** 2-bedroom listings offer the highest ROI relative to acquisition costs.

---

## 2. Business Context
Albany's market is driven by "The Hill" (State Government), multiple universities, and its role as a gateway to the Adirondacks. For investors, the challenge is navigating the seasonal fluctuations of a government-centric city.

---

## 3. Problem Statement
How can new and existing hosts optimize their pricing and amenity offerings to maximize revenue while maintaining high guest ratings in a mid-sized urban market?

---

## 4. Data Strategy
We utilized raw listing data from Inside Airbnb, covering:
*   **Cleaning:** Removing outliers (e.g., $10,000/night placeholder prices) and normalizing currency.
*   **Feature Engineering:** Calculating **Estimated Revenue** and **Occupancy Rates** using the San Francisco Model (review-based).
*   **KPIs:** Focusing on RevPAR (Revenue Per Available Room) as the primary success metric.

---

## 5. Statistical Analysis & Insights
Our team conducted several hypothesis tests to validate common assumptions:
1.  **Host Experience:** Is there a significant difference in ratings between Superhosts and regulars? (Result: Yes, statistically significant p < 0.05).
2.  **Room Type Impact:** Do "Entire Homes" truly generate 3x the revenue of "Private Rooms"? (Result: Confirmed, but with higher overhead costs).

---

## 6. Tableau Visualization
We developed three specialized dashboards to tell the story of Albany's data:
1.  **Market Heatmap:** Identifying high-density clusters.
2.  **Revenue Deep-Dive:** Correlations between price bins and booking frequency.
3.  **Quality Metrics:** Analyzing host response times vs. review scores.

---

## 7. Strategic Recommendations
1.  **Investment:** Focus on "Second Ward" and "Sixth Ward" for the best balance of price and demand.
2.  **Operations:** Automate guest communication to maintain the <1 hour response rate required for Superhost status.
3.  **Amenities:** In Albany, **High-Speed Wifi** and **Dedicated Workspaces** are the top-converting amenities due to the high volume of business/government travelers.

---

## 8. Conclusion
SmartStay Analytics provides a roadmap for data-driven hospitality in Albany. By shifting from "gut feeling" to "data insight," hosts can significantly improve their margins and guest experiences.
